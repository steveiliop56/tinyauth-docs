    Tinyauth

    Tinyauth is the easiest way to secure your apps with a login screen. Forget endless configuration and complex dashboards and say hello to a five-minute installation.

    Get StartedDemo

    Simple

    Tinyauth focuses on simplicity. It is designed to only require environment variables for configuration and works out of the box without the need for fancy dashboards or configuration files.

    Lightweight

    Tinyauth ships as a single statically linked binary with no dependencies and requires practically no resources to run.

    OAuth and LDAP support

    With Tinyauth you can you can easily login to your apps using your favorite OAuth providers or by using a centralized LDAP server.
